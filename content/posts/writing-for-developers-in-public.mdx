## Clarity beats performance theater

Technical readers notice when the writing is more interested in sounding smart than helping them think.

A good editorial system should support precision and warmth at the same time.

## Structure is part of trust

Scannable sections, stable metadata, and well-separated callouts help readers decide how deeply to invest.

Design should help that decision happen quickly.
